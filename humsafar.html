Humsafar

Farhat Ishtiaq

“Humsafar” Farhat Ishtiaq ki ek bohot popular, emotional aur romantic novel hai. Yeh kahani Ashar aur Khirad ke rishte ki hai — ek arranged marriage jo dheere dheere pyar mein badal jata hai. Novel mein family politics, misunderstandings, ego aur mohabbat ke darmiyan hone wale conflicts ko bahut achhe se dikhaya gaya hai.

* Ashar: Ego aur Family Pressure

Ashar ek ameer aur successful aadmi hai jo family pressure ki wajah se Khirad se shaadi karta hai. Shuru mein woh Khirad ko accept nahi karta aur unke rishte mein bohot misunderstandings hote hain.

* Khirad: Sabr aur Mohabbat

Khirad ek simple, educated aur sabr wali ladki hai. Woh Ashar ke ghar mein bohot mushkilon ka saamna karti hai lekin apne usoolon aur imaan par qaayam rehti hai.

"Rishte banane mein waqt lagta hai, aur todne mein ek pal bhi kaafi hota hai."

— Humsafar

>>> Arranged Marriage aur Shuruaat

Ashar aur Khirad ki shaadi family ke pressure mein hoti hai. Dono ke darmiyan shuru se hi misunderstandings paida ho jate hain. Ashar Khirad ko apni wife ke taur par accept nahi karta.

>>> Family Politics aur Conflicts

Ashar ke ghar mein bohot family politics hoti hai. Khirad ko wahan adjust karna bahut mushkil hota hai. Woh apne sabr aur izzat se sab mushkilon ka saamna karti hai.

>>> Mohabbat ka Badlao

Dheere dheere Ashar Khirad ki khubsurati, sabr aur character ko samajhta hai. Unke rishte mein pyar paida hota hai lekin bohot mushkilon ke baad.

>>> Separation aur Reunion

Novel mein bohot bada separation hota hai. Ashar aur Khirad alag ho jate hain. Phir ek waqt aata hai jab dono ek dusre ki ahmiyat samajhte hain.

Key Themes:

  • ◈Arranged Marriage
  • ◈Family Politics
  • ◈Sabr aur Mohabbat

Title Meaning:

"Humsafar" ka matlab hai saath safar karne wala. Yeh novel do logon ke rishte ko dikhati hai jo waqt ke saath ek dusre ke humsafar ban jate hain.

"Rishte waqt ke saath bante hain, aur pyar waqt ke saath gehra hota hai."
