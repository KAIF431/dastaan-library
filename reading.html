Chapter 01

Salar Sikandar ka IQ level 150 tha. Wo ek aisa ladka tha jo apni zindagi ki talash mein bhatak raha tha. Use lagta tha ke duniya ki har cheez uski mutthi mein hai, magar sukoon... sukoon usse kohon door tha.

Dushwar raaste hamesha sukoon ki taraf nahi le jate, kabhi kabhi wo sirf andhere ki gehrai badha dete hain. Imama Hashim ke liye hidayat ka rasta itna asaan nahi tha. Usne qurbani di thi, aisi qurbani jo har koi nahi de sakta.

"Allah ne har bhatke hue insan ke liye ek raasta rakha hai, bas humein wo 'Peer-e-Kamil' mil jaye jo hath tham kar roshni ki taraf le jaye."

Salar ne jab pehli baar maut ko chakhna chaha, toh use nahi pata tha ke ye maut nahi, balki ek nayi zindagi ka aghaz hone wala hai. Wo dastan jo Salar se shuru hui thi, ab poori kainaat ka hissa banne ja rahi thi.
