Umera Ahmed

Chapter 01

Turkey ki barfili galiyan aur Istanbul ki thandi hawayein ek mysterious raaz ko chupaye hue thin.

Haya Suleman ke liye ye sirf ek educational trip nahi tha, balki uski puri shakhsiyat ka badlav tha.

Ek taraf 'Major Ahmed' ka saaya tha, aur dusri taraf 'Jannat ke Patte' yaani wo Hijab jo Haya ke liye uski hifazat ka nishan ban gaya. Isme spying hai, suspense hai, aur ek aisa ishq jo dunya se shuru hokar Rab tak jata hai.

Jihan Sikandar ke kirdar ne humein sikhaya ke har nazar aane wali cheez haqiqat nahi hoti.

Chapter ki baaki dastan yahan aayegi...
