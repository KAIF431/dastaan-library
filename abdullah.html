Abdullah

Hashim Nadeem

“Abdullah” Hashim Nadeem ki ek bohot gehri, roohani aur inspirational novel hai. Yeh kahani ek aise insaan ki hai jo duniya ki sabse badi azmaishon ke bawajood apne imaan, sabr aur Allah par yaqeen ko kabhi nahi chhodta. Novel mein sacrifice, taqdeer aur asli sukoon ki talash ko bahut powerful tareeke se dikhaya gaya hai.

* Abdullah: Imaan ka Pahad

Abdullah ek aisa aadmi hai jo zindagi ki har mushkil mein Allah par poora bharosa rakhta hai. Woh material duniya ko chhod kar asli sukoon ki talash mein nikalta hai.

* Sacrifice aur Taqdeer

Novel mein uske personal losses, family se doori aur bohot badi qurbaniyon ko dikhaya gaya hai. Phir bhi woh apne imaan par mazboot rehta hai.

"Jab insaan Allah par poora bharosa kar leta hai, toh duniya uske liye chhoti ho jati hai."

— Abdullah

>>> Abdullah ka Safar

Abdullah ek aam insaan hai jo zindagi ki normal raftar mein chal raha hota hai. Lekin ek din uski zindagi mein aisa waqiya hota hai jo uske imaan ko parakhne wala sabse bada imtehan ban jata hai.

>>> Imaan ki Azmaish

Woh apni family, career aur duniyavi cheezon ko chhod kar sirf Allah ki raza ki talash mein nikalta hai. Har step par uske imaan ko bohot badi azmaishon ka saamna karna padta hai.

>>> Sacrifice aur Sabr

Novel mein uske bohot bade sacrifices dikhaye gaye hain — family se doori, logon ki nafrat aur material losses. Phir bhi woh kabhi Allah se shikwa nahi karta.

>>> Asli Sukoon ki Talash

Aakhir mein Abdullah ko ehsaas hota hai ke asli sukoon sirf Allah ki raza mein hai, duniya ki kisi cheez mein nahi.

Key Themes:

  • ◈Imaan aur Yaqeen
  • ◈Sacrifice aur Sabr
  • ◈Asli Sukoon

Title Meaning:

"Abdullah" ka matlab hai "Allah ka banda". Yeh novel us insaan ki kahani hai jo sachcha Abdullah banne ki koshish karta hai.

"Duniya sab kuch le sakti hai, lekin imaan nahi chheen sakti."
