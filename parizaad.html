Parizaad

Hashim Nadeem

“Parizaad” Hashim Nadeem ki ek bohot powerful, emotional aur social novel hai. Yeh kahani Parizaad naam ke ek kaale rang ke ladke ki hai jo apni zindagi mein bohot badi nafrat, rejection aur society ke zulm ka shikar hota hai, lekin apne talent, mehnat aur imaan ke bal par upar uthne ki koshish karta hai.

* Parizaad: Rejection aur Struggle

Parizaad ek kaale rang ka ladka hai jise society uske rang ki wajah se reject karti hai. Woh bohot talented hai lekin har jagah uska mazak udaya jata hai.

* Talent aur Imaan ki Taqat

Novel mein dikhaya gaya hai ke Parizaad apne singing talent aur mehnat ke bal par upar uthne ki koshish karta hai. Uske andar ka imaan aur sabr uski sabse badi taqat hai.

"Rang se nahi, dil se insaan ko pehchana jata hai."

— Parizaad

>>> Parizaad ki Struggle

Parizaad bachpan se hi apne kaale rang ki wajah se logon ki nafrat aur mazak ka shikar hota hai. Woh apne sapnon ko pura karne ki koshish karta hai lekin har jagah rejection milta hai.

>>> Talent ki Talash

Woh apne singing talent ko pehchanta hai aur usko polish karne ki koshish karta hai. Lekin society uske rang ke aage uske talent ko nahi dekhti.

>>> Imaan aur Himmat

Parizaad apne imaan aur himmat ke bal par mushkilon ka saamna karta hai. Woh kabhi haar nahi manta aur apne maqsad ki taraf badhta rehta hai.

>>> Success aur Realization

Aakhir mein uski mehnat rang lati hai. Novel batati hai ke asli success rang, paisa ya looks se nahi, balki dil aur mehnat se milti hai.

Key Themes:

  • ◈Racism aur Rejection
  • ◈Talent aur Mehnat
  • ◈Imaan aur Himmat

Title Meaning:

"Parizaad" ka matlab hai "Pari jaisa". Yeh novel us ladke ki kahani hai jo bahar se kaala hai lekin andar se pari jaisa khoobsurat dil rakhta hai.

"Duniya rang dekhti hai, lekin Allah dil dekhta hai."
