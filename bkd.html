Bachpan Ka December

Hashim Nadeem

“Bachpan Ka December” Hashim Nadeem ki ek bohot sensitive, emotional aur nostalgic novel hai. Yeh kahani bachpan ki yaadon, masoomiyat, family ke rishton aur waqt ke guzarne ke baad hone wale badlao par markaz karti hai. Novel mein December ki thand aur bachpan ki khushiyan ek saath juda hua ehsaas bahut khoobsurati se bayan kiya gaya hai.

* Bachpan ki Yaadein

Novel bachpan ke us golden period ko yaad dilati hai jab zindagi simple thi, rishte sachche the aur December ki thand mein bhi dil garam rehta tha.

* Waqt ka Badlao aur Dard

Bachpan ke baad jab waqt guzarta hai, toh family ke rishte badal jate hain, log alag ho jate hain aur woh masoomiyat khatam ho jati hai. Novel is dard ko bahut gehrai se mehsoos karati hai.

"Bachpan ka December kabhi wapas nahi aata."

— Bachpan Ka December

>>> Bachpan ki Masoomiyat

Novel bachpan ke un golden days ko yaad dilati hai jab zindagi mein koi tension nahi hoti thi. December ki thand mein bhi ghar mein garam ehsaas hota tha.

>>> Family ke Rishte

Novel mein maa, baba, bhai-behen aur dada-dadi ke rishton ko bahut pyar aur gehrai se dikhaya gaya hai. Woh rishte jo bachpan mein sabse strong hote hain.

>>> Waqt ka Guzarna aur Badlao

Jab bachpan khatam hota hai aur log bade ho jate hain, toh rishte badal jate hain. Log apne apne raaste chun lete hain aur purane din sirf yaadon mein reh jate hain.

>>> Nostalgia aur Dard

Novel mein December ki thand ke saath saath dil ki thand ko bhi mehsoos karaya gaya hai. Yeh batati hai ke bachpan kabhi wapas nahi aata.

Key Themes:

  • ◈Bachpan ki Masoomiyat
  • ◈Family Rishte
  • ◈Nostalgia aur Waqt ka Guzarna

Title Meaning:

"Bachpan Ka December" ka matlab hai bachpan ki woh thandi aur pyari yaadein jo kabhi wapas nahi aati. December yahan sirf mausam nahi, balki bachpan ka ehsaas hai.

"Bachpan sirf ek umar nahi, ek ehsaas hai jo kabhi wapas nahi aata."
